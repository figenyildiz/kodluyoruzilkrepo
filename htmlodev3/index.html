Practical and Rich Chocolate Sauce Cake

8-10 People | 30 Min Preparation - 30 Min Cooking

Materials:

For Cake:
4 eggs
  • 1 of glass sugar
  • 1 teacup of milk
  • 1 teacup of oil
  • 1 packet of vanilla
  • 1 packet of baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons of full cocoa
  • 1 glass of flour

    • For Whipped Cream
  • 1 bag of whipped cream
  • 1 glass of cold milk

    • For the Chocolate Sauce
  • 1 package of powdered chocolate sauce
  • 2.5 cups of milk
  • 1 pack of dark chocolate (70 g)


    • Fabrication

    For the cake, we mix the eggs and sugar. Add milk, oil, cocoa, vanilla, baking powder and flour and mix.
    We throw it into the oven, which we heated to 160 degrees. We take the baked cake out of the oven and wait for it to cool.
    We prepare whipped cream and chocolate sauce. On the cooled cake, we pour the whipped cream first and then the chocolate sauce. Enjoy your meal...
